Privacy Policy for Theme Inspector

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Theme Inspector collects the following information:

  • Email address: When you sign in with Google or subscribe to our service
  • Website data: Information about Shopify themes on websites you visit (theme name, price, features)
  • Usage data: Whether you've used the free trial and subscription status

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide theme analysis and detection services
  • Manage your subscription and billing through Stripe
  • Track your free trial usage
  • Improve our service

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Google Sheets: For subscription management and user data
  • Stripe: For payment processing and billing
  • Chrome Storage: For local extension data (email, subscription status)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For user authentication
  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Google Apps Script: For backend processing

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers (Google, Stripe) as necessary for our service

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
